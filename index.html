üìñ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How do I request a new mobile device?

Submit a ticket via Rubix using the following options> ‚Ä¢ Ticket Type: Do you want us to do something new or make a change to how something works? ‚Ä¢ Ticket area: Hardware ‚Ä¢ Category: Smartphone. For a new device/SIM, use the following: ‚Ä¢ Sub category: Request New Mobile Device/SIM This initiates an automated form for completion and relevant cost centre and approval to be attached.

Can I make personal calls or use mobile data?

No. All usage is tracked via weekly/monthly reports and must comply with council policy.

Can I sign in with my Apple ID or iTunes account?

No. Linking personal accounts is not permitted on council-issued devices.

What apps are available by default?

Apps like Outlook, Teams, Company Portal, GoBright, Authenicator and Whatsapp are included. Some roles may have extra apps pre-installed.

How do I request an app not listed?

Log a Rubix request including the app name, App Store/Play Store link, business justification, and note if the app is free or paid. The security team will review your request.

What if I lose or damage my phone?

Log a ticket via Rubix. If lost/stolen, include a police report reference. We will attempt a remote wipe and issue a replacement if needed.

How do I factory reset my phone?

WARNING: All files and data will be wiped. To factory reset the device - Go to Settings> Transfer or Reset iPhone> Click Erase All Content and Settings> Click- Confirm.

How do I find the serial number of the phone?

Go to Settings> General> About> You will find the the Serial number here.
